Cup & Saucer

This cup and saucer was collected by Tom Joseph,a Pilot for the Bay and River Delaware from vessels he guided up and down the river. It is very rare that shipping companies still create such china and rarer still for a pilot to be presented with a memento like this today. At the beginning of the 21st century, only some Scandanavian shipping lines still practice this tradition.
